This is the miniature version of Alison.

Alison is a living biological entity of the class Mammalia in the order of Primates and belonging to the family Hominidae. As such, she is also designated to the genus Homo and the species Homo sapiens sapiens Linnaeus.

She began her Homo sapien journey in Lakeland, Florida. Today, she finds herself scavanging the prarie lands of Oklahoma City.

But there were many stops along the way. The following is an exciting list of locations where Alison has lived and worked. Let's see, there was:

  • Tampa, FL
  • Colorado Springs, CO
  • Brandon, FL
  • Lavonia, GA
  • Lakeland, FL (again)
  • Greenville, SC

And now she's here improving her programming skills with OK Coders.
