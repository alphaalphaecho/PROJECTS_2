This is Alison in the Emergency Room.

And this is pretty much how she looks after she's been jogging.

But this day's particular jogging excursion was quite different.

During her jog, Alison was chased by 3 large, angry dogs that bolted out from behind a thick layer of trees.

Startled, Alison sprinted back toward her car as fast as she could. On the way, she lost her balance and dove into the asphalt like she was sliding into first base.

Her gashes, gouges, bruises, and rotator-cuff injury are better now.
And the dogs never got her!
